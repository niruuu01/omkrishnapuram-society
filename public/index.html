About Our Society

Omkrishnapuram Co-op Housing Society Ltd. is a vibrant residential community located in the heart of Shahad (W). Our society stands as a testament to cooperative living, where residents come together to create a harmonious and progressive environment.

Established with the vision of providing quality housing and fostering community spirit, we have grown into a well-managed society that prioritizes the welfare and development of all our members. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our operations.
